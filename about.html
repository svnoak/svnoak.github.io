About Practical Privacy Guide

What is a pracitcal privacy guide?

The practical privacy guide is like the name says: a guide that is focusing on the practical parts on privacy. With this guide I want to empower internet users to protect their own privacy online, and to create a pedagogical guide to make it as easy as possible.

Who made this guide?

This guide was made by Kim, someone who simply knows enough to make a practical guide.

Why should I trust this guide?

Like our parents said when we millenials started using the internet:
“Don’t trust anything you read on the internet.”
I am simply someone who is working with webdevelopment and pedagogics and have an idea about that society can only walk towards a sustainable future if we are allowed to remain free in mind and soul. This is only possible de-commercialisation of us as people - both in real life and online.
I do not claim to know everything or be the guiding light regarding online privacy.
I have simply been a person who people have come to for advice regarding tech. This is only my way of sharing my knowledge.
